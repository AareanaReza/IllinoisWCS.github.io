Project

This year's WCS tech team will be focusing on human biometrics, starting off utilizing the Polar H7 heart rate strap API! We'll be working with bluetooth packets, integration with desktop applications and web, while touching many important concepts in cloud architecture, full stack development, database management, networking, and any other aspects that members want to approach for the first time. We welcome all genders, brand new CS-majors, to seasoned programmers to join and come work in a warm awesome motivated group on this project! Don't be shy, as we are looking to helping, teaching, working with, and learning from students from freshman to grad students.

We will meetings every Thursday until the end of Fall Semester in Siebel Center 4403 from 7 - 8:30pm. New meeting times for next semester will be announced shortly!

High School Visits

We will be conducting high school visits in late September, early October to talk to high schoolers in the area about Chictech, our upcoming CS retreat. We will need volunteers to help answer questions and lead activities that serve as a good introduction to what studying CS has to offer and what it's all about! If you are interested in participating, please fill out the Chictech volunteer survey.

Chictech Retreat

Chictech is our annual retreat for high school girls to gain exposure to computer science. We hold technical workshops for all experience levels, as well as fun student-led mentoring and social activities. Find more information here.

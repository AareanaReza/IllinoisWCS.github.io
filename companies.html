2014-2015 Opportunities

Sponsorships

WCS holds an annual awards banquet in April to recognize our active members & the companies have provided support to our organization. Each sponsorship receives an invite for 2 representatives to attend our annual banquet.

Bronze ($100) Company name in Program
Silver ($250) Company name and logo in program
Gold ($500) Company name and logo in program as well as on a poster at the banquet. Donations greater than $500 will simply be denoted as Gold+.

Donate online at http://cs.illinois.edu/giving/give-now or email illinoiswcs@gmail.com .

Event Opportunities

General Meetings [Email to check available months]
Description: WCS holds monthly general meetings where we first give our announcements and then have a company speak about the company and any topic as desired. We handle advertising through our newsletter and social media posts, make the order for dinner + drinks, bring paper plates, eating and serving utensils, napkins, and any other supplies.
Duration: 1.5 hours
Target Audience: All WCS Members
Average Cost: ~$350
Responsibility: Inviting guest speakers. Bringing giveaways are optional and can help with audience involvement.

Info Sessions [Email to schedule]
Description: Hold an informational meeting introducing your company and then diving deeper in a related tech talk.
We handle advertising through our newsletter and social media posts, make the order for dinner + drinks, bring paper plates, eating and serving utensils, napkins, and any other supplies.
Duration: 1 hour
Target Audience: All WCS Members
Average Cost: ~$300
Responsibility: Inviting guest speakers. Bringing giveaways are optional and can help with audience involvement.
